Mom & Me:Twinning

The relationship of a Mother with her Child is so pure and here we at Flames, celebrates this relationship with ramp walk based on theme of twinning. The Mom and Me(Child) has to dress accordingly with the same kind of outfit or carry same colours on the ramp.
There are 2 sub-categories in this based on the age of child:
1. Upto 6 years
2. From 6 to 12 years
